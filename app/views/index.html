Further iterations

For a period several features were prototyped on the HMPPS DPS platform to connect to various case management system applications like NDelius, OASys, ARN and CVL. The structure of those data sources formed part of the constraints for design and user experience. Below are those features;

  • Generation of the Recall Part A
  • Generation of the Decision not to Recall
  • Redesign of licence conditions
  • Redesign of licence history into contact history
  • Presentation of risk information from OASys
  • Approach for displaying offence and sentence information

Read the Design History to understand the work behind those features.
